Handmade minature
since 2000
Miniature craftsmanship is a unique and fascinating art form. It is the creation of small objects with great attention to detail. Miniature crafts can be made from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, clay, glass, and fabric. They can be realistic or abstract, whimsical or serious. But no matter what material or style they are made from, miniature crafts are all works of art.
